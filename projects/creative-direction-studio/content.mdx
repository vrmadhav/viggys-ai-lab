## What this explores

Most image tools begin with a prompt box. Creative Direction Studio begins earlier: with the incomplete idea, the tension inside the brief, and the choices that determine whether an image communicates.

The studio maintains an editable Working Intent, proposes three conceptually distinct directions, generates a preview for each, and supports selection, rejection, combination, decision locks, revision history, and branches.

The prompt is still available for inspection, but it is compiled from the creative decisions rather than treated as the product.
