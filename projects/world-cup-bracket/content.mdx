## The idea

World Cup Bracket is a compact knockout-stage tracker for the 2026 FIFA World Cup.
It loads the latest match feed when the page opens, locks completed games, and
keeps upcoming matches clickable so you can play out possible paths to the final.

## What updates

- Match teams, venues, dates, status, scores, and winners refresh from the live
  scoreboard feed.
- Completed games are locked automatically.
- If the feed is unavailable, the bracket falls back to the embedded snapshot
  instead of rendering empty.

## Try it

Open the app, pick winners for upcoming games, and watch the later rounds update.
Use refresh when a match changes while the page is already open.
